Retention Strategy

Customer Segments

Champion

Profile:
- Highest RFM scores
- Highest spending levels
- Strong website engagement
- Lowest churn rate (7%)

Recommended Action:
 VIP rewards
Early access to new products
Loyalty benefits
Referral campaigns

Expected Value:
Safeguards the highest value customers and boosts their lifetime value.



Loyal Customer

Profile:
Strong purchase history
 Consistent spending
 Moderate churn rate (30%)

Recommended Action:
 Cross-sell recommendations
 Personalized product suggestions
 Loyalty point incentives

Expected Value:
Promotes repeat sales and upsells purchases.


High Value Unhappy

Profile:
 Spending customers who have very high spending.
 Faster spin speed than Superstars
 Support interactions present
 Churn rate (23%)

Recommended Action:
 Excellent website features
 Complaint resolution follow-up
 Personalized retention offers

Expected Value:
Retains valuable high-value customers.



Discount Sensitive

Profile:
 Frequent discount usage
 Lower spending levels
 Churn rate (64%)

Recommended Action:
 Targeted coupons
 Bundle offers
 Limited-time discounts

Expected Value:
Boosts retention and holds promotion expenses in check.



Dormant

Profile:
 Low engagement
 Low purchase activity
 Churn rate (49%)

Recommended Action:
 Re-engagement campaigns
 Product reminders
 Win-back email sequences

Expected Value:
Recovers customers prior to them becoming inactive.



At Risk

Profile:
 Very high recency
 Lowest purchase frequency
 Lowest spending
 Highest churn rate (90%)

Recommended Action:
 Immediate win-back campaign
 Personalized outreach
 Strong retention incentives

Expected Value:
Greatest opportunity to decrease churn.



Budget Prioritization

In the case of a restricted retention budget, customer groups should be ordered in this way:

1. At Risk
2. High Value Unhappy
3. Loyal Customer
4. Discount Sensitive
5. Dormant
6. Champion

Reasoning:

At Risk customers have the greatest likelihood of churning.

High Value Unhappy customers are a significant revenue source and must be kept before they move on.

Loyal Customers offer excellent Future Value and sometimes can be retained at a lower cost.
