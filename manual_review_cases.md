# Manual Review Cases

## Purpose

The following customers were selected for manual review to validate segmentation quality and identify opportunities for targeted retention actions.

---

### 1. CUST02038

**Segment:** At Risk

**Reason for Review:**

* Extremely high recency (262 days)
* No purchases in the last 180 days
* No monetary contribution
* Minimal engagement

**Recommended Action:**
Immediate win-back campaign or removal from active marketing lists if inactive for an extended period.
